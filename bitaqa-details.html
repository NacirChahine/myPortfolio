Description

Bitaqa is a comprehensive e-business card solution designed for companies to create and manage the business cards of their employees efficiently. With Bitaqa, businesses can digitize their networking efforts, streamline contact information management, and ensure consistency across employee profiles. Simplify networking, enhance professionalism, and make lasting impressions with Bitaqa.
