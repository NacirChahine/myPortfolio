Description

Qareb is an innovative e-commerce platform designed exclusively for the fishing community in Saudi Arabia, with plans for expansion across the GCC and MENA region. Offering a wide range of payment options, including settlement services like Tabby and Tamara, as well as flexible shipment choices, Qareb aims to provide a seamless and tailored shopping experience for fishing enthusiasts.
